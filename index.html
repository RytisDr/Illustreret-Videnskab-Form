About this bag

Get your very own insidious partner for the bike ride, hiking or sailing trip.
The rugged Backpack from Outpack is 100% waterproof and keeps your valuables dry in all weather. Even if the accident is out and you lose your backpack in a rushing river, you can expect the contents to not get wet.
The bag is made of strong PVC material and assembled with extra strong welds for a perfect seal. The roll closure at the top makes it easy for you to access your valuables, while keeping water out. Due to the waterproof material of the backpack, it is particularly suitable for, for example, electronics.
On the front of the bag you will find a smart holder that you can use to hold a helmet or sleeping bag for example.
